Code of Conduct

Guidelines for respectful, safe and responsible participation across Mpumalanga Local Time.

Effective: 6 July 2026

Respectful Communication

Interact respectfully. Avoid abusive language, personal attacks, or harassment. Treat contributors and readers with dignity.

No Hate Speech

We prohibit hate speech targeting protected characteristics including race, gender, religion, sexual orientation, or nationality.

No Racism

Racial slurs or discriminatory content will be removed and may lead to account suspension.

No Bullying or Harassment

Persistent harassment, doxxing, or targeting of individuals is prohibited.

No Misinformation or Fake News

Do not intentionally share false or misleading information. Verify facts before posting.

No Illegal Activities

Content facilitating illegal activities is prohibited and will be removed.

Responsible Sharing

Share responsibly; respect intellectual property and privacy of others.

Protect Privacy

Do not publish private or sensitive personal data about third parties without consent.

Reporting Abuse

If you encounter abuse, report it to our moderation team at moderators@mplocaltime.co.za with a link and description.

Moderator Rights & Consequences

Moderators may remove content, warn users, suspend or ban accounts for violations. Repeated violations may lead to permanent suspension.

Community Values

We value thoughtful journalism, civil discourse, and local voices. Contribute constructively and help maintain a positive environment.

FAQs

How do I report abuse?
Email moderators@mplocaltime.co.za with details and links.
What happens after I report?
Our moderation team will review and respond as appropriate, typically within 48 hours.
