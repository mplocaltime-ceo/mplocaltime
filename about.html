About us

Delivering trusted local news for Mpumalanga.

Our mission is to connect communities with accurate reporting, civic accountability and stories that celebrate local life.

Our editorial commitment

We focus on stories that matter to everyday readers: neighbourhood events, regional business developments, sports coverage, arts and culture, and social issues impacting Mpumalanga.

Local focus

Our coverage is rooted in Mpumalanga. We publish local news, community features and business updates that serve residents across the province.

Our values

  • Accuracy
  • Transparency
  • Accessibility
  • Community engagement

Contact and contribution

If you have a story, tip or event to share, we welcome contributions from across Mpumalanga. Reach out through our contact page to tell us what matters in your neighbourhood.
