Accessibility

Designed for clear, readable local news.

We are committed to accessibility, legible typography and simple navigation for readers across Mpumalanga.

Accessibility commitments

  • Semantic HTML and meaningful headings.
  • Keyboard-friendly navigation and visible focus states.
  • High contrast text, readable fonts and responsive design.
  • Alt text for images and clear link labels.
